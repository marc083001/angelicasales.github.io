THAILAND

Thailand is one of the world's top tourist destinations, with its vibrant culture, beautiful beaches, and breathtaking natural landscapes.
One of the most famous tourist spots in Thailand is the Grand Palace. The Grand Palace, also known as the Wat Phra Kaew, is a
symbol of Thai monarchy and Buddhism. It serves as the official residence of the King of Thailand and is one of the most significant buildings in Thailand..


Ko Phi Phi, Phang Nga

This beautiful island is famous for its white sand beaches and turquoise waters. Ko Phi Phi is surrounded by lush green jungle and offers stunning views of the
limestone karst formations that are the iconic feature of Phang Nga Bay.



The Grand Palace, Bangkok

A stunning architectural masterpiece, the Grand Palace is situated in the heart of Bangkok and serves as the official residence of the Thai Royal Family.



The Floating Market, Chiang Rai, Thailand

 Located in the northwestern part of Thailand, this unique market features a floating system of wooden stalls,
with each stall anchored to the shore by bamboo poles.
